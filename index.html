What have I recently been up to?

I am currently a software engineering intern at Qu Platforms Inc. Qu Platforms is a web application that allows online content creators to charge their followers a small fee in exchange for an answer to a question. I have utilized Typescript and React.js to add multiple features to the website, such as utilizing Google Firebase to add a panel that shows payouts to content creators and a self-onboarding process for new content creators.

I am also the Solve It With Code First Year Interest Group Assistant. I am a mentor to a group of first year computer science students and I teach them problem solving skills, code debugging, and study habits.

I was research assistant at Carnegie Mellon from June 2023 to August 2023. I designed a curriculum add-on for the AP Computer Science Principles course that included code examples and practice problems with solutions.
